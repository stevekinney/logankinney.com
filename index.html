Logan Kinney, RYT

Glow and Flow Yoga, LLC

Follow @yogabeachnbaby

Logan Kinney completed her yoga teacher training at Yoga to the People, in 2011. She shares their philosophy that there are no proper clothes, glorified teachers, or gurus … just yoga, accessible to all bodies! She also trained to teach at Rockaway Beach Hot Yoga.

In addition, Logan recieved her Kids Yoga certification from Om Schooled in NYC, and has a master’s degree in childhood education, concentrating in special education. She has taught classes at Yoga to the People in Brooklyn, and the Lower East Side, independently in Harlem and Hoboken, NJ, and in Jersey City at Yoga in the Heights. She is currently pursuing her prenatal yoga certification through YogaWorks.

Private Lessons

Private lessons are available upon request, in the location of your choice. She will work with you at your level, to achieve your personal goals. Whether you are looking to relax or get in shape, she will help you do both!

Preparation for Birth

Sign up with me for a 5 class intensive, where we will discuss your birth plan, and how yoga can make that plan a reality. The journey of pregnancy and birth is transformational, and yoga can help you gain physical and emotional strength as you grow along the way!
